FAVORITE RECIPES created In beersmith:

STONE IPA (5 GAL)

Grain:

    13# 2 Row Pale Malt

    1/2# Crystal Malt 10L

    1/2# Crystal Malt 20L

Hops:

    1oz Magnum (75 min. boil)

    1oz Centennial (Flameout)

    2oz Centennial (Dry Hop)

Yeast:

White Labs Cal Ale Yeast (WLP001)-1800ml Starter

MASH/SPARGE/BOIL

    Mash at 150F to 152F for 60 mins.

    Boil for 75 mins.

    Cool and ferment at 66F to 68F
